Our Story:

How cupcake heaven came into being.

Cupcake heaven was once an idea that occurred to a simple baker out of Bakersfield. "What if," he thought, "there was a place where you didn't have to choose between great cupcake flavors because you could eat a little of every one?"

He brought this idea forward to the Fraternal Order of Free Bakers one night and was laughed out of the lodge.

Rather than swearing revenge and carrying out a lengthy program of targeted assassinations, he decided to prove them wrong by opening just such a restaurant. And so was born Cupcake Heaven!
